CELSIN - KFI

Christian Education Leadership and School Improvement Network

A Christian School Development Program of Kingdom Foundation Institute, Liberia Inc. built around five service pillars for connecting K-12 Christian schools, sharing best practices, facilitating collaboration, and offering peer support.

Program Contact:
Email: info@kingdomfoundationinstituteinc.org

Five Service Pillars

  • K-12 Christian Schools Networking
  • Annual Christian Education Summit
  • School Improvement Consultancy
  • K-12 School Accreditation & Recognition Program
  • Professional Development Training

Fees Structure

Fees are based on service scope, school size, number of participants, delivery location, and support duration. Contact the Admissions Office for an official quotation.

Registration

Schools may register interest through the CELSIN-KFI academic services page or contact the office directly by email, phone, or WhatsApp.
